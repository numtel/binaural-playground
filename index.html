Sequence Format

You must wear headphones!

Type the sequence of steps in the text area.

Each line must contain exactly 3 numbers separated by commas:

  • Timestamp (in milliseconds,
    or using units like '1h 2m 30s' or '35s')
  • Left channel frequency (Hz)
  • Right channel frequency (Hz)

Frequencies shift linearly between steps.

Frequencies may include decimals.

Binaural Frequency Guide

The experienced binaural beat is the difference between channels.

  • 1-4 Hz: Delta (Deep sleep)
  • 4-8 Hz: Theta (Meditation/REM)
  • 8-14 Hz: Alpha (Relaxation)
  • 14-30 Hz: Beta (Focus/Energy)
  • 30-100 Hz: Gamma (Maximum Awareness)
